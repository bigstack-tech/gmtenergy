Our track record with a proven history of delivering exceptional results

We succeed when our clients succeed. GMT is proud to have consistently grown its business and market reputation as a trusted and reliable support to the global and local operators who we work with.

Following the passing of the Nigerian Content Act and the establishment of the Nigerian Content Development and Monitoring Board (NCDMB) in 2010, the nation set itself on to a path of more local participation in, and ownership of, its vast oil and gas industry. GMT strives to be an outstanding exemplar of this path of local content empowerment.

Our established organizational structure, which includes partnerships with global and local consortia, ensures that we are well positioned to deliver proficient solutions and services at optimum value to clients and customers in the offshore oil & gas sector.
