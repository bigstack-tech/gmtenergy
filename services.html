OPERATIONAL FACILITIES

FABRICATION YARD & MARINE LOGISTICS BASE - WARRI & PORT HARCOURT

GMT has a partnership with logistics base and various Fabrication yard located out of Port Harcourt, Rivers State, and Warri that can offer seamless onshore and offshore logistics support to the oil and gas industry. The base enables fabrication, sandblasting, equipment handling, load-out of large structures, and many more.

  • The yard size is 150,000 meters square with a jetty front and quay of 230 meters long.
  • The water draft is 7.5 meters at low tide and at high tide is about 8.5 meters.
  • Installed storage facilities up to 3500m3 of AGO.
  • 500m3 of potable water replenished by two wells at 100m3/Hr.
  • Office Building.
  • Stacking area with access by 8.0m wide concrete roads.
  • Large covered warehouse area.
